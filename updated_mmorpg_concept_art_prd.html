# MMORPG Concept Art Catalog: "Relics of Rune & Iron" ## Project Overview A comprehensive library of 2D concept art for a semi-realistic fantasy MMORPG, blending the gritty, dark atmosphere of *Diablo 2* with the iconic, recognizable item progression of *RuneScape*. ## Visual Direction - **Style:** Semi-realistic. High detail on materials (pitted iron, worn leather, woven fabric) but with slightly emphasized silhouettes for readability. - **Atmosphere:** Gritty, dark fantasy. Earthy tones, oxidized metals, and weathered textures. - **Technical Requirement:** All designs must be presented on a clear, transparent background to facilitate 3D modeling pipelines. ## Dynamic Management System - **Create New Category:** Ability to add custom categories (e.g., "Dolche", "Pets"). - **Modular Upload:** Support for uploading individual item concepts (Gloves, Helmets, etc.). - **Export Pipeline:** One-click export for 3D generation. ## Catalog Structure (30 Concepts per Category) ### 1.
