# MMORPG Concept Art Catalog: "Relics of Rune & Iron" ## Project Overview A comprehensive library of 2D concept art for a semi-realistic fantasy MMORPG, blending the gritty, dark atmosphere of *Diablo 2* with the iconic, recognizable item progression of *RuneScape*. ## Visual Direction - **Style:** Semi-realistic. High detail on materials (pitted iron, worn leather, woven fabric) but with slightly emphasized silhouettes for readability. - **Atmosphere:** Gritty, dark fantasy. Earthy tones, oxidized metals, and weathered textures. - **Technical Requirement:** All designs must be presented on a clear, transparent background to facilitate 3D modeling pipelines. ## Catalog Structure (30 Concepts per Category) ### 1. Plate Armor (Tier: High/Heavy) - **Components:** Helmet, Chest Armor, Shoulder Pads, Gloves, Pants, Boots. - **Focus:** Fluted steel, ornate engravings, heavy layered plating. ### 2. Heavy Armor (Tier: Mid/Soldier) - **Components:** Helmet, Chest Armor, Shoulder Pads, Gloves, Pants, Boots. - **Focus:** Chainmail underlays, reinforced leather, pragmatic iron slabs. ### 3. Leather Armor (Tier: Scout/Rogue) - **Components:** Cowl/Hood, Tunic, Spaulders, Bracers, Leggings, Boots. - **Focus:** Treated hides, buckles, straps, animal furs, and dark dyes. ### 4. Cloth Armor (Tier: Mage/Scholar) - **Components:** Hood/Circlet, Robes, Mantle, Cuffs, Skirt/Trousers, Sandals/Shoes. - **Focus:** Elaborate embroidery, layered silks, arcane symbols, and ragged hems. ### 5. Swords (One-Handed) - **Focus:** Arming swords, falchions, and longswords. Variety in hilts and pommels. ### 6. Two-Handed Swords - **Focus:** Greatswords, claymores, and zweihanders. Imposing scale and weight. ### 7. Axes (One-Handed) - **Focus:** Hatchets, war axes, and bearded axes. ### 8. Two-Handed Axes - **Focus:** Battleaxes, greataxes, and double-headed executioner axes. ### 9. Sword and Shield (Paired Sets) - **Focus:** Matching visual themes between the blade and the buckler/heater/tower shield. ### 10. Magic Wands - **Focus:** Gnarled wood, crystalline cores, bound artifacts, and floating elements. ### 11. Spears - **Focus:** Partisans, glaives, pikes, and winged spears. --- *Status: Initializing Catalog Generation...*
