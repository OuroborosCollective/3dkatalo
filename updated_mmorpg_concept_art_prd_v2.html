# MMORPG Concept Art Catalog: "Relics of Rune & Iron" ## Project Overview A comprehensive library of 2D concept art for a semi-realistic fantasy MMORPG, blending the gritty, dark atmosphere of *Diablo 2* with the iconic, recognizable item progression of *RuneScape*. ## Visual Direction - **Style:** Semi-realistic. High detail on materials (pitted iron, worn leather, stone, wood) but with slightly emphasized silhouettes for readability. - **Atmosphere:** Gritty, dark fantasy. Earthy tones, weathered textures, and atmospheric lighting. - **Technical Requirement:** All designs presented on clear backgrounds. Now supports linking and embedding finished 3D models (e.g., .GLB). ## Dynamic Management System - **Category Creation:** Dynamic expansion for armor, weapons, NPCs, and now Architecture. - **3D Asset Pipeline:** Each concept item now includes a "Link 3D Asset" or "View 3D Model" feature, allowing for seamless transition from 2D concept to 3D implementation. - **Modular Upload:** Continued support for individual components (Helmets, etc.) and architectural elements. ## Catalog Structure (30 Concepts per Category) ### 1.
