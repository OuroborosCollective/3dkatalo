# How to Select and Export Your Designs To select screens on the Stitch canvas: 1. **Single Selection:** Click once on any screen to select it. 2. **Multiple Selection:** Hold down the **Shift** key and click on multiple screens to select a group. 3. **Select All:** You can often click and drag your cursor over an area to select multiple items at once. ### Exporting for Your App: Once you have selected one or more screens, you have several options in the top toolbar: * **" View Code":** This is the most important for your no-code agent. It gives you the full HTML and CSS for the selected screen(s). * **"Export to Figma":** If you want to refine the designs in a professional design tool. * **"Download Screenshot":** To get a high-quality image of the current view. **Pro Tip:** For your no-code agent, I recommend selecting the **Global 3D Pipeline Dashboard** ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_70}}) and the **Master Asset & Code Manifest** ({{DATA:TEXT:TEXT_33}}). These provide the logic and the roadmap they'll need to build the full app.
